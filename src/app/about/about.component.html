Auditions Round 1

The first round is your first impression and a ticket to go ahead in achieving your dreams. We check your ability and talent and the courage and determination to go ahead. This is the easiest round where you just have to prove yourself.

Auditions Round 2

In second round, we judge you on the basis of your talent and only a few selected ones get the ticket to semi finale. You have to prepare yourself properly for this round as this is not that easy as of round 1. Here, you have to show your flair and compete with others to go ahead.

Semi Finals

Just a limited number of kids get to this round, who have talent, skills and abilities in the field for which they are competing. If you perform and compete well then only you will be qualified for the finals. Just 4-5 kids will go ahead.

Grand Finale

We will stick together till the end. The grand finale will be the final way to showcase all of that you have; best of yours because in this stage, you will be titled as a winner. All your efforts so far will be given colours in this final round. Just 4-5 kids will get a chance to come here in this round and hence, they must be bestest of all so far.
